About Me

My Journey

I’m here to help young people who feel stuck, unsure, or overwhelmed by life’s challenges.

Whether it’s navigating career choices, building meaningful relationships, or simply finding peace within, I’m passionate about creating safe spaces where you feel seen, heard, and valued.

People often tell me I make them feel at ease, allowing them to share their emotions openly and walk away feeling lighter and more empowered. For a long time, I didn’t fully recognize this quality in myself, I never gave that comment enough thought or attention. It wasn’t until I went through a rough patch in my own life and walked myself through it, finding clarity and strength along the way, that I realized my true calling: to guide others toward inner peace, confidence, and self-love.

I’m Aya—a youth life coach who turns insight into action. I pair mindful listening with practical tools so every session moves you forward with calm and confidence.

Vision

I envision a peaceful and compassionate world, where every young person embraces inner peace, loves themselves fully, and extends that love to others.

Mission

Inspiring young people to unlock their full potential by nurturing self-awareness, building confidence, and enhancing emotional well-being with practical tools and a safe space for personal growth.

Qualifications

My professional background and certifications.

  • Master’s Degree in Strategic Human Resources – University of Huddersfield, UK.
  • Certified Professional Life Coach – Transformation Academy, USA development.
  • 5+ Years of Experience in Youth Empowerment – Specializing in:
    • Professional growth & career development

  • Practical, Empathetic, and Transformational Approach

If you’re ready to explore your potential, let’s connect—I’d love to walk with you on your journey to growth and self-discovery.

Values that matter
